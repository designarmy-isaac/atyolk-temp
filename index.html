SHAED Music Video Shoot ATYOLK

ATYOLK serves Washington, DC with a 10,000 square foot studio for photography, video, and events.

ATYOLK is a creative space for creative minds. We are design professionals and we built ATYOLK from the ground up to our own exact specifications. With an 18-foot cyclorama, abundant daylight, and soaring 25-foot ceilings, we offer the best photo and video studio for rent in Washington, DC, Maryland, and Virginia.

The three studio psaces, drive-in workshop, two kitchens, and interior balcony lounge are infinitely configurable to accomodate any project. This is Washington, DC's largest black canvas. The only limit to what you can create here is your imagination.

Studios

We know what you need to get your work done. Our photography studio is infinitely flexible, with movable walls, full eastern and southern exposures, polished concrete floors, a driveable workshop and cyclorama wall, elevated grids, high-speed internet, and 600 Amp 2 Phase Camlock power. And we have the extras and amenities to take your shoot from productive to inspired: elevated client lounge, full kitchens and kitchenettes, hair & makeup stations, bathroom with shower and towels, coffee service, exclusive catering options, and an extensive list of local production specialists and partner vendors. Contact us to find out how we can help.

Mission

AT YOLK exists to support and nurture the creative community in Washington, DC. Our mission is to provide a place for creators to meet, create, and share their vision and knowledge. Alive and dynamic, AT YOLK is a living environment, a continuous work in progress. We are ever evolving to meet the needs of a community of creators. We look to inspire, to connect creative minds, to mentor, to collaborate, and to teach those who dream of creating their own culture.
